About | The TARTARUS Initiative

Tactical Arms Race Treatise Against R of all United Sovereignties, or TARTARUS, is a special accord amongst several of the allied sovereign nations following the end of WWII and unveiling during the Cold War. Its effects can be seen in the instances of Operation Freedom Banner and the CERBERUS weapons program as the motto "Any Means Necessary", or its less charitable "Peace through Blood", echos throughout its history.
